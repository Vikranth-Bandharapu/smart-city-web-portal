How to Access & Download Official Notices

Follow these simple steps to access, view, and download official government documents securely from our portal. This guide applies to all types of notices, orders, and policy circulars.

1. Find the Notice

Scroll through the list of published notices or use the search feature to locate the document you need.

2. Preview the Document

Click “View Full Document” to read the notice. If available online, you can preview it instantly in your browser.

3. Download for Offline Use

Choose the download icon on the viewer page to save a copy in PDF format to your device.

4. Print or Share

If you require a hard copy, use the print option. You may also share the document link with others.
